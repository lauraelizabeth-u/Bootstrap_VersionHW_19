About Me

Hi! My name is Laura Elizabeth, I’m a UX/UI designer currently based in Miami, FL. I’m especially passionate about UX Research and Writing. I like to take inspiration for my design work from my interests in psychology, art, and nature. Before entering the technology field, I spent years working in childcare, refining my empathy and communication skills. I graduated from the UX/UI Bootcamp in the University of Central Florida in 2022. When I’m not creating you can find me dancing at a concert, sitting in a bookshop or enjoying the sun at the beach.
