The Problem

Travel apps don’t provide enough information for users’ liking, so they’re often left dissatisfied and feeling unsafe, which results in a lower rate of returning customers.

The Solution

To improve the app by including virtual tours, safety guidelines and other details so users can make a more informed decision regarding their travel.

Project Description

Odysseia is a post covid travel app designed to help users plan their travels while staying on a budget and being mindful of cleanliness and Covid Guidelines.

Team Members:

Laura Isaac, Maria Telles, Leon Dinh, Ashley Stanford

Role:

UX & UI Designer

Tools:

Figma, Trello, Slack, Google Drive

Empathize & Define

As per the Design Thinking process, we began by empathizing with potential users hoping to understand their problems, concerns, gains, and goals, to help them as best as we can. Then, with the information we collected we set out to define what problem that the majority of users are facing we will target with our design.

User Interviews

To begin our process we designed an interview that covered user’s research, planning, and what they consider most important when planning a trip. Then, we put our script into practice with 5 people who fit our target audience.
